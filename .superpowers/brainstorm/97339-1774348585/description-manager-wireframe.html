What happens at each step
Step 2 – Match SKUs: Each row is matched against the products DB. Existing SKUs get updated; new SKUs are flagged for creation + enrichment.
Step 3 – AI Process: Claude rewrites/validates EN + TH descriptions, extracts flavour notes, taste profile, and visual cues — all stored back per product.
Step 4 – Review & Publish: Side-by-side before/after per product. You approve or skip each item before committing to the database.
New SKUs: Auto-queued through the existing enrichment pipeline (country, region, classification) after descriptions are accepted.
